RECOMB 2008
 

PAPER SUBMISSION

Submission procedure:

Submission deadline due on 12:00 Noon GMT

Submissions are done through the easychair conference system
http://www.easychair.org/RECOMB2008

If you don't have an easychair account yet, please register to obtain one.

Once logged onto the system, you can enter the author information and upload your manuscript in pdf format.

You will also find a button that lets you choose whether your submission shall be considered for publication in the Genome Research Recomb issue or not. Furthermore, please fill in the topics that apply to your submission.

Submission deadline is September 24, 12:00 Noon GMT.
